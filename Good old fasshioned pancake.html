GOOD OLD FASHIONED PANCAKE

DESCRIPTION

This is afast food recipe for breakfast meals taken mostly by people in Africa most the Eastern part of the continent.

This is a nutrition meal which will ensure yor stomath is full the whole morning.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablesppon of butter
  • 4 cups of baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 cup milk

STEPS

  1. In a bowl, sift together the flour, bakking powder,salt and sugar. Make awell in the center and power in the milk,egg and melted butter
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over mediam-high heat. Pour the batter onto the griddle. Brown on both side and serve hot.
