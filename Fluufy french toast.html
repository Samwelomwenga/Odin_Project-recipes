Flufy French toast

DESCRIPTION

This is a nutrition french break first meal taken by poeple living in paris

This one of the sweetest dishes dishes in the worlds

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup all- purpose flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 table spoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices of bread

STEPS

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl.Slowly whisk in the milk . whisk in the salt , eggs,cinnamo, vanillaextra and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled grilled or frying pan over mediam fat.
  3. Soak bread slices in the mixture until saturated. cook bread on each side until golden brown .Seave hot.
